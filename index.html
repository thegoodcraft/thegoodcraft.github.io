Mission

The Good Craft is a pop-up market showcasing the work of an evolving group of local artists from the Bay Area.


We encourage and empower new yet talented artists to show their work in a lively, fun, and collaborative environment.


The Good Craft’s mission is twofold: to create an accessible space for new and talented artists to showcase their work, and to support our community by donating to a rotating local non-profit.
